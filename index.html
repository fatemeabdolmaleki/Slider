Harry Potter, an orphaned boy, learns on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. There, he befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and together they encounter magical creatures, spells, and dark forces. Harry learns he is linked to Lord Voldemort, the dark wizard who killed his parents. Throughout his years at Hogwarts, he grows stronger while facing many dangers. Ultimately, Harry fights to defeat Voldemort and protect the wizarding world from his evil.
